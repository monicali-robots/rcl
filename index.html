About

The Contact Robotics Lab at the University of Washington studies robot interaction with the physical world. Our research combines mechanical design, tactile sensing, manipulation, soft robotics, and machine learning to enable robots to perform robust real-world tasks.

Research

Tactile Sensing

Novel sensors for measuring touch, contact forces, and interaction dynamics.

Robot Manipulation

Robust grasping and object interaction in cluttered environments.

Mechanical Design

New end-effectors and robotic hardware inspired by physical interaction.

Soft Robotics

Compliant mechanisms and adaptive robotic systems.
